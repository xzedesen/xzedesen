Introduction

Welcome to Xzedesen ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our verified social networking platform.

By accessing or using Xzedesen, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

Important: As a verified social network, we maintain higher standards of data protection and require additional verification information to ensure platform authenticity and security.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect the following personal information during registration and account verification:

  • Basic Profile Information: Full name, username, email address, password
  • Demographic Information: Gender, date of birth, location (state and city)
  • Profile Photo: Uploaded or captured through your device's camera
  • Verification Data: Information required for account verification purposes

Automatically Collected Information

When you use our services, we automatically collect:

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device type
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform
  • Technical Data: Cookies, log files, and similar technologies

Verification Information

For platform integrity and verification purposes, we may collect:

  • Government-issued ID information (only for verification, not stored permanently)
  • Biometric data (optional, for enhanced security features)
  • Additional documentation required for account verification

How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses

  • Account Creation and Management: To create and maintain your verified account
  • Platform Security: To prevent fraud, abuse, and ensure community safety
  • User Verification: To maintain our verified user community standards
  • Communication: To send important updates, security alerts, and administrative messages
  • Personalization: To customize your experience and show relevant content

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal information based on:

  • Contractual Necessity: To provide the services you requested
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our services and ensure platform security
  • Consent: For specific purposes where we obtain your explicit consent

Data Sharing and Disclosure

When We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share specific information
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help us operate our platform
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Safety and Security: To protect our rights, users, or the public

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can view your information
  • Regular Audits: Security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Employee Training: Staff training on data protection and privacy
  • Incident Response: Procedures for handling data breaches

Note: While we implement robust security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your information
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a usable format
  • Right to Object: Object to certain processing activities

Account Settings

Through your account settings, you can:

  • Update your profile information
  • Adjust privacy settings
  • Manage notification preferences
  • Download your data
  • Deactivate or delete your account

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze how our services are used
  • Prevent fraudulent activity
  • Improve user experience

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our platform.

Children's Privacy

Xzedesen is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete such information.

If you believe we might have any information from or about a child under 18, please contact us immediately.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services to you
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our agreements

When we no longer need to retain your information, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

Third-Party Links and Services

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites or services. This Privacy Policy does not apply to those third-party sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notify you through our platform or via email
  • Provide a summary of significant changes

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about our information practices.

Contact Us

  • Response Time: We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 48 hours
